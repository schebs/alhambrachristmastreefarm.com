Sustainable Growing

Celebrating with a real Christmas tree has a significant environmental benefit. A real tree spends around 8 years growing in the field before it is harvested. During that time, it produces oxygen, provides a habitat for wildlife, and preserves green space. And unlike artificial alternatives, a real tree is 100% recyclable and biodegradable. To conserve water resources, all of our locally grown trees are on drip irrigation.

Meet the Family

Mark and Bea and their children, Wyatt and Ellie, took over the farm from the Griffin and Jones families in 2014 with the goal of keeping alive the tradition and legacy of the farm, which is one of the last operating farms in Alhambra Valley and the only remaining Christmas Tree Farm in Contra Costa County.

We all have a deep love for the farm, which is also our home, and are excited to continue the tradition that started over 55 years ago! Our commitment to quality and personal service has made us a holiday destination for thousands of families.

A Legacy of Community: We are committed to hiring local high school and college students and supporting local artisans. Each year we donate a portion of our sales to a local non-profit organization. Recent recipients have included The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society and the Food Bank of Contra Costa & Solano.
