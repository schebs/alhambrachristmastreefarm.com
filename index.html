Meet the Scheberies Family

Mark, Bea, Wyatt, and Ellie - dedicated to bringing joy to your holidays

A Legacy of Excellence

The Alhambra Christmas Tree Farm is a family-owned business. Mark, Bea and their children Wyatt and Ellie have been operating the farm for over ten years, growing several varieties of Christmas trees. We are committed to hiring local high school and college students, while being deeply committed to the Bay Area community.

We all have a deep love for the farm and are excited to continue the tradition that started over 55 years ago! Our commitment to quality and personal service has made us a holiday destination for thousands of families.

Every tree is selected with care, every customer is treated like family, and every holiday season brings our family joy as we help create your perfect Christmas memories.
