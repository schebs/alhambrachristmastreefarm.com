About Our Family Farm

Over 55 Years of Christmas Magic Since 1969

The Alhambra Christmas Tree Farm is a family-owned business where Mark, Bea and their children Wyatt and Ellie have been creating unforgettable holiday experiences for families throughout the Bay Area.

Our Story

For over 55 years, Alhambra Valley Christmas Tree Farm has offered the very best quality Christmas Trees, Wreaths and Garland on the Market. What started as a dream in 1969 has grown into one of the Bay Area's most cherished Christmas tree destinations in the beautiful Alhambra Valley of Martinez.

Mark, Bea and their children Wyatt and Ellie have been operating the farm for over ten years, growing several varieties of Christmas trees. We are committed to hiring local high school and college students, while being deeply committed to the Bay Area community.

We all have a deep love for the farm and are excited to continue the tradition that started over 55 years ago! Our commitment to quality, personal service, and creating magical Christmas memories continues with the entire Scheberies family.

Sustainable Practices

We believe in caring for our land and environment. Our sustainable farming practices ensure that future generations can continue to enjoy fresh, beautiful Christmas trees.

  • Integrated pest management to minimize chemical use
  • Soil conservation and improvement programs
  • Water conservation through efficient irrigation
  • Wildlife habitat preservation on our property
  • Recycling programs for tree disposal after holidays
  • Renewable energy use where possible
  • Sustainable transportation for tree delivery
